This is how the band was formed. Not so long ago, we held our music degrees in our hands and asked, "What on earth are we going to do with a music degree?" "Hey, let's form a band and see where it takes us!!" So we did. We put everything into recreating the well-known and timeless music that we grew up with. Music that everyone LOVES!
